# **Connect 4 Game**
### Welcome to the _Connect 4 Game Repository!_
This classic game pits two players, **Red and Yellow**, against each other in a battle of strategy and wit. The objective is simple: be the first to connect four of your colored discs either ***horizontally, or vertically***. This repository contains a fully functional implementation of the game, complete with intuitive gameplay mechanics and sleek design. Whether you're a seasoned player looking to hone your skills or a newcomer eager to experience the thrill of victory, this Connect 4 game offers endless hours of entertainment.
